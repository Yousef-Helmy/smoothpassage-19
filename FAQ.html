Frequently Asked Questions

General

Smooth passage is the road toll system of the Integrated Transport Centre - Department of Municipalities and Transport in Egypt and it works without kiosks or barriers.

When passing through the smooth passage toll gates during the Peak Hours, all vehicles are identified through the plate number and the data is sent to the smooth passage system and processed. The approved policies and regulations for the Toll Gates are applied.

There is no need to purchase an external tag and apply it to the vehicle windshield.

Toll gates are located on main roads leading to governorates and crowded areas.

The toll fees will be collected from the users according to the roles and regulations. The toll fees will be collected automatically from the financial wallet of the vehicle for every pass under the gates.

Registration

The registration requirements are as follows:
  1. Vehicle license plate number.
  2. The Egyptian ID of the account holder.
  3. An active personal email if you are registering a personal account.
  4. Mobile number registered with the traffic file number.

Account

From the login page, you have to press on "forgot password" option and fill in your registered email. Then the system will send you a link by email to reset your password.

Yes, you have to log in with your registered account, then click on your photo on the top right of the page to navigate to the personal profile page and press "edit profile information." Update your profile details and press "done" to confirm the update.

Wallet

You can top up your wallet by logging into your account, clicking on the "wallet" button in the control panel at the bottom of the screen, then selecting 'Recharge', and choosing your preferred payment method from the three options: (Visa, electronic wallet, Fawry).

You must recharge your wallet within five working days, otherwise, you will receive an email of a fine.
